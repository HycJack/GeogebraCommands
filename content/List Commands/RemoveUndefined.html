RemoveUndefined Command

RemoveUndefined( <List> )

Removes undefined objects from a list.

RemoveUndefined(Sequence((-1)^k, k, -3, -1, 0.5)) removes the second and fourth element of the sequence since expressions \((-1)^{1.5}\) and \((-1)^{2.5}\) are undefined and yields list {-1, 1, -1}.

See also Remove Command.
